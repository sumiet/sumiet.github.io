  • Rostra’14 is a team-cum-individual event.
  • Youth Parliament, Vaad Vivaad, Spell-Bee , Mythical Extempore ,Quick Flix , Super Heroes are individual events.
  • Marcos , Panchayati Raj and News Room are team events.
  • The team shall consist of 6 participants.
  • Getting yourself registered on the website , individually or as a team , is a pre-requisite for participation in any of the events at Rostra’14.
  • All the participants have to be present at the registration desk on 7th March , 2014 during inaugration to receive the registration slips , failing which you will not be allowed to participate in any of the events.
  • Each team can reserve one participant for the event YOUTH-PARLIAMENT as per to their liking. Thus they can effectively take part in Team events with 5 members also.
  • For individual events, a team can have only one of the members to represent the team. The other members of the team, if they wish to participate, shall have to get themselves registered individually for the particular event.
  • Note: If a participant is a member of the team, but has taken part as an individual in an event, and wins it; he shall be treated as an individual participant only.The winning points will not be carried forward for the team tally.
  • The Registration fees for Rostra’14 is as follows:
    • Team Registration- Rs. 800 for a team of 6.
    • Individual Registration for a single event- Rs. 50/-
    • Individual Registration for multiple events- Rs. 100/-
    • Registrations for Youth Parliament end on 5th of March, 2014. Rest of the events have on the spot registrations also.
  • The members of the participating teams may have to make certain arrangements according to the schedule of the simultaneous events. Kindly decide your team as per the event schedule.
  • Everyone willing to take accommodation for Rostra’14 must register on website.
  • The participant should fill the accommodation form given on the website. Last Date for filling the application form is 4th March, 2014.
  • Accommodation would be confirmed latest by 5th March . A confirmation mail will be sent on your email-id.
  • When you arrive at BIT Mesra, you need to come to the Accommodation Desk along with your college identity card. You will be allotted your place of stay on campus.Accommodation would be strictly on shared basis.
  • In case of any disputes, decision of Hospitality CGs would be final and binding.
  • Accommodation charges are Rs. 150 per head.
  • What type of accommodation will be provided? Accommodation will be provided to boys and girls in well-guarded separate hostels on the campus of BIT Mesra.
  • What are the accommodation charges for Rostra 2014? An amount of Rs.250/- per person is accommodation fee for all four days in which Rs. 100/- is refundable amount.
  • In exceptional circumstances the refund amount may be forfeited subject to one or more of the following conditions:
    • Permanent damage has been done to the room/dorm/accessories where accommodation has been provided.
    • Rooms are not vacated on or before 10th March 1000hrs.
  • Due to limited space available, Accommodation would be given to participants on preferential basis.
  • Are separate arrangements made for accommodation of professors? Separate accommodation arrangements for faculty are subjected to availability and cannot be guaranteed.

    • For any queries and doubts, feel free to contact the Hospitality Team.
    *The charges will be the same irrespective of duration of stay up to a maximum of four days i.e. from 7th March to 10th March .

Birla Institute of Technology,mesra is one of the most respected, celebrated and oldest institutions of the country, founded in 1955, by the renowned industrialist-philanthropist B.M. Birla. Besides being a pioneer in numerous technical, educational and cultural domains, BIT has been incessantly embarking on various cultural undertakings like the advanced fine arts, eastern and western classical music, dance and one of the most proficient and veteran knacks of all time, Literature!

The Literary Society of BIT, Mesra embodied by a group of enthusiastic individuals, seeks to reconcile the otherwise estranged dimensions of literature and public speaking. Earlier known as Speak BIT Movement, ROSTRA ‘14 is a step forward towards providing an adequate platform to individuals with varying intellect and sheer wit, in order to create a true representation of the Literary expertise.
